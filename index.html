Repeater

Big signal. Local roots.

The Blowtorch of Parma is the Jim Snell, W8DRZ, Memorial Repeater. It operates from the WJW Channel 8 TV transmitter tower near State Road and West Pleasant Valley Road in Parma.

On air: 444.050 MHz • +5 MHz offset • 131.8 Hz tone

About the station

The repeater uses a commercial Motorola MTR-2000 and is described by the club as pushing 124 watts to an antenna positioned 321 feet above the ground.

Whether you are a long-time ham or just getting started, listen in, check in, and meet the operators behind the voices.
