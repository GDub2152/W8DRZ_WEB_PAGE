About the Club

Modern home for a classic Northeast Ohio radio club.

Founded in 1935, THE BLOWTORCH OF PARMA Amateur Radio Club promotes the art, science, and enjoyment of amateur radio through education, service, and fellowship.

Club focus

Weekly on-air activity, technical learning, radio experimentation, public service, and helping new operators get comfortable on the air.

Repeater

W8DRZ Jim Snell Memorial Repeater

The club repeater operates from the WJW TV 8 tower area in Parma, Ohio.

Program your radio: 444.050 MHz output • +5 MHz offset • 131.8 Hz PL tone

Quick details

  • Callsign: W8DRZ
  • Output: 444.050 MHz
  • Offset: +5 MHz
  • PL tone: 131.8 Hz
  • Weekly net: Monday evenings at 8:00 PM
